R & D - Research & Development - Manufacturing

With our rich experience and deep understanding of technology, backed by our own production capabilities and centralized local resources, we offer a comprehensive one-stop service for small to mass production that can be customized to your needs.

Leveraging Machine-to-Machine (M2M) communication, we specialize in turnkey product development from idea to outcome, catering to various sectors such as wearable technology, smart meters, and other Internet of Things (IoT) devices. Our expertise extends beyond product development as we provide manufacturing services that cover all stages of the product life cycle, including design modifications, updates, and component end-of-life support.

Application Firmware

Our comprehensive firmware development services provide companies with the tools they need to get the most out of their hardware. Our services allow them to control gadgets and equipment remotely, collect and process sensor data, and connect devices within an IoT system. Our team of experts has deep domain experience and offers reliable firmware development services, including system architecture design, device driver development, power optimization, OTA upgrade support, and more. With our services, companies can ensure their hardware reaches its maximum potential.

Manufacturing

Our manufacturing facility offers top-notch electronic manufacturing services for a wide range of high-quality electronic assemblies for various industrial sectors. The state-of-the-art shop floor is equipped with the latest technology.

Our experienced team will help integrate the prototype into the equipment and test its effectiveness. Once approved by the client, we will start mass production. With our expertise in electronic product design and production capabilities, we provide comprehensive solutions tailored to your specific needs. From concept to production, we ensure seamless execution, delivering high-quality IoT devices built to perform well.
