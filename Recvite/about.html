A commitment to excellence

Connecting people to the right opportunities

Chartswood is a leading company in Singapore, founded in 2015 to provide value-based recruitment services, Internet of Things (IoT products), Artificial Intelligence services, Machine Learning Services, and Mobile Application Development services to companies globally. We exploit the power of advanced technologies to augment our clients' businesses. As part of our adherence to satisfying the necessities of our clients, we are continuously innovating and extending our services. It is our honor to have functioned with some of the world's top enterprises, and we desire to deliver a feasible service to our customers. Our major drive is to build long-term relationships with our clients and to check whether they are satisfied with our services.


Quality Recruitment Services

Finding, attracting, and maintaining top talent is always a difficult task for any business, as the focus of the companies will usually be on hiring a good team. If you are exploring a reliable recruitment agency for your staffing needs, you're in the right place. At Chartswood staffing services, we esteem our clients the most, which means being genuine about how we can assist them in finding the right staff for their business or organization. That could be temporary staffing solutions, permanent staffing solutions, or even executive searches for those hard-to-find positions that require expert attention from an experienced recruiter who understands your industry.


How we Hire

Our recruitment process starts with collecting prerequisites for a job and then proceeds with sourcing candidates from our vast database, conducting headhunting processes, background verification, reference checks, and so on. We provide customized recruiting solutions to meet the specific requirements of businesses ranging from large MNCs to small businesses.


Our Product Development Process

Our first step is to come up with an idea and then conduct market research to see if there is a demand for it. Then, we develop a prototype and check if it meets customer needs. Once the prototype is confirmed, the product will be designed and developed based on the client's feedback. We then launch the product after thorough testing and provide continuous support after the product launch.
