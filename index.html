ABOUT US

Elevate your lifestyle with intelligent technology

Voni SmartIoT revolutionizes product development and R&D solutions to turn any impossible ideas into real-life products using technologies,fostering innovations within the ecosystem Our synergistic approach to IoT solutions benefits everyone's lifestyle by dealing with the current and future challenges in the real world with an optimal smart switch for home automation.

Through innovation and IoT product design, VONI’s expertise extends to home automation, healthcare, smart agriculture, transportation, and smart cities. It helps sustain mutual growth and provides citizens and businesses with better services through technology.

Our experienced team of engineers possesses an effective combination of the latest technology skills, domain knowledge, and methodologies to deliver high-quality customized touch switches. Our deep understanding of the latest scientific discoveries and novel technologies keeps us at the forefront of this highly competitive market.

Voni SmartIoT is a preeminent and leading smart switch manufacturer developing and distributing quality-assured, personalized touch switches at affordable prices. We are one of the leading smart switch exporters, and our smart switch boards are made of premium quality raw materials that meet global quality parameters.
