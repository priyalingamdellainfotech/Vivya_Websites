Smartap

With Smartap device, We can make your ordinary electrical appliances into smart appliances in minutes. The Smartap replaces traditional switchboard and allow control of appliances and lights using smart gadgets such as phones, Tablets and Voice Assistant. They can be used to turn appliances on/off, control fan speed and Dimmable lights.

Highlights
  • Can be installed in existing and new homes without any wiring changes/Re-wiring.
  • Easy and quick to install. Make your home smart within 10 minutes.
  • Schedule your devices to turn on/off automatically at pre-fixed times.
  • Backlight for each touchpads
  • Providing USB type-C and USB Type-A connectors to charge your mobile phone, Tablet, power Bank and Laptops without power Adaptors, delivering up to 60 Watts of power.
  • Providing Universal 3-Pin Socket for international socket compatibility.
  • Reduces energy wastage.
  • Supports all major smart home ecosystems: Amazon Alexa, Google Home and Apple HomeKit
  • Control and monitor your home from anywhere in the world using all your smart gadgets.
  • Providing in-built magical tool that makes any 3 rd party home appliances(non-Smart device) into smart without any physical changes or integration. of power.
  • Can be installed in any standard Electrical backbox/concealed box.
