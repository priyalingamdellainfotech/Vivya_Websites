What is a Statement of Work?

A Statement of Work is a document that details the parameters and expectations of a specific project or task. This document outlines the project's objectives, deliverables, timelines, and other pertinent information. An SOW specifies the expectations of the customer, the obligations of the service provider, and the criteria for success.

An effective Statement of Work provides a clear roadmap for all stakeholders and ensures alignment during the project management process.
