Privacy Policy

Welcome to Valiantt info solution . We specialize in providing comprehensive background and drug test verification services. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to the responsible collection, use, and protection of personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary for background checks and drug testing. This may include names, dates of birth, social security numbers, addresses, employment history, educational backgrounds, and criminal records.

For drug testing purposes, we may collect health-related information which is considered highly sensitive. This includes test results and medical history where relevant.

We may also collect non-personal information about the way our services are accessed and used, including but not limited to, browser names, types of computers, and technical information about users' means of connection to our website.

Use of Collected Information

Information collected is primarily used for conducting background checks and drug testing as requested by our clients.

We use collected information to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Providing customer service and support.

To improve our website and services based on the information and feedback we receive from you.

We take the security of personal information very seriously. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information.

"In the app, we don't collect or store the scanning information in our database." This ensures that any scanning data processed through the app is not saved or maintained in the company's database, thereby protecting user privacy and reducing the risk of unauthorized access to sensitive information.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers.

Third-Party Websites

Our website may include links to other websites. We do not control and are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the updated date at the top of this page. We encourage users to frequently check this page for any changes.

Collection and Use of Location Data

We use location data solely for the purpose of verifying employment backgrounds. The location data captured through the app is used in real-time to validate the information provided by candidates during background checks.

Face Data Handling

Explicit Statement on Face Data Retention : Valiantt Location Tracker does not retain any face data. We do not store, share, or process face data beyond the immediate requirement for verification.

Reasons for Storing Face Data : We do not store face data. Any face data captured is used solely for the real-time verification process and is not retained after the verification is complete.

Length of Time Face Data is Stored : Face data is not stored at all. It is used in real-time and discarded immediately after the verification process.

Third Parties and Face Data : We do not share face data with any third parties. Since we do not store or retain face data, there is no sharing or processing by third parties.

Reasons for Sharing Face Data with Third Parties : As we do not share face data with third parties, this point is not applicable.

Third Parties Storing Face Data : Since we do not share face data with any third parties, no third parties store or retain face data.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
