Product Description

Our SmarTack smart switch acts as a gateway to the world of smart IoT products. With our Siri-supported smart switch features, you can control your home automation devices using voice commands. SmarTack's compact size fits easily into your standard 4M electric box and makes physical switches intelligent. With VONI app integration, you can access all your smart appliances on the go.

Our VONI smart Wi-Fi touch switches are the perfect example of Smart IoT products, supporting 15 Amp load and dimmer arrangements. You can set your mood lighting or ceiling fans according to your preferences and get ready for a comfortable movie experience just by swiping the dimmer on the app. The VONI customized smart switches come equipped with schedulers so that every light, air conditioner, geyser, and curtain in your home operates seamlessly.
