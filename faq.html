FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive mail?

If you receive an offer from a staffing agency, the company will initiate a background verification process for you. Subsequently, you will receive an email to complete the necessary background checks.

Why do I receive more emails?

The designated onboarding team will conduct additional background checks. Whenever you receive a new username, please use the most recent one to continue the process.

Why is my screen blank/white during the second time filling?

This issue may stem from cache problems on your website. To resolve this, consider the following solutions:

  • Use a different browser.
  • Copy the link and open it in an incognito window.

What is the Valiantt Tracker App?

The Valiantt Tracker application is utilized for verifying both temporary and permanent addresses. It records your location and transmits this information to the background verification team

What is skip button is not visible second time ?

Should the onboarding organization require additional information from you, the "Skip" button will be disabled on your form. This data will subsequently be verified by Valiantt Info.

Why cannot I Log in second time to Valiantt tracker Application?

Why forget password feature is not working?

The "Forgot Password" feature may not work because of your browser cache. Try these steps:

  • Remove browser data/cache.
  • Copy the "Forgot Password" link from the email and paste it in incognito mode.
  • Try using a different browser or device.

Can I use the Valiantt Tracker on my iPhone?

Yes, the Valiantt Tracker application works well on both Android and iPhone.

Can I use the Valiantt Tracker on my Android Phone?

Yes, the Valiantt Tracker application works well on both Android and iPhone.

Why are my login credentials expired?

The login credential message may be displayed under the following circumstances:

  • If you do not attend to the message within 7 days and attempt to access the link after this period, your login credentials will expire.
  • You may be using outdated login credentials. Please ensure you are using the most recent username provided.
