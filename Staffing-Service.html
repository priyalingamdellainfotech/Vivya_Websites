Vendor Management System Services

Automate and speed up the administrative processes with Della's integrated VMS solutions, which help customers to fill job requests faster than their competitors. Our VMS Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) application distributes job requirements to staffing agencies, recruiters, consultants, and other specialized vendors, reducing the complexity of using many vendors. The main features of our VMS services include

  • Risk mitigation
  • Performance managementt
  • Supplier onboarding
  • Financial compliance management
  • Applicant Tracking Function
  • SLA Management Software
  • Candidate Relationship Management (CRM) tools
