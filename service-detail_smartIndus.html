Smart Industrial energy management system

Challenges

Energy costs in the manufacturing industry can be as high as 30% or more and continuously rise.

Lack of visibility into electricity consumption.

No tracking of power shutdowns.

Continuously turning on electrical appliances can cause damage.

Solution

Wireless sensors will be installed on electric machines/standard meters.

The sensors will continuously monitor the power consumption of the machines.

Provide behavioral change alerts to increase user awareness.

Data will be posted in real-time to the cloud.

Benefits

24/7 real-time monitoring.

Integration with standard energy meters.

Alerts for behavioral changes in machines for the user.

Customized dashboards.

Data for monitoring the electrical equipment.

Schedule lights and electrical appliances to turn ON/OFF as required.
