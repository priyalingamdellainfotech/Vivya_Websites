TERMS & CONDITIONS

Project Cost
An approximate estimate is given to the customer when a customer makes a booking with us. The actual project cost varies with the super builtup area and finalized architect designs.
Advance Payment
15% of the overall project value will be collected as advance payment. This amount is paid to confirm the booking which will be utilized for the survey, architectural designs, finalising and blocking the vendors / contractors and the materials.
Package cost
Our standard package rates are also subjected to variations based on the rate of tiles, marble granite, plywood, laminates, plumbing fixtures for interiors etc at the time of construction.
Refund policy
Booking amount is non refundable as the amount would have already been utilized for designing, survey, freezing vendor and handling charges.
Client scope
Electricity, Bore and water will be charged extra and the customer is accountable for all these related payments and for ensuring timely supply of water and electricity at the site.
