ABOUT US

Our Story

A subsidiary of the VR Della Group of Companies, Voni SmartIoT creates advanced IoT products utilizing cutting-edge artificial intelligence, machine learning, and deep learning. Our skilled team of engineers combines technological expertise, industry knowledge, and processes to develop customized solutions that streamline processes, reduce inefficiencies, and improve overall performance.

We offer R&D and product development services across a broad range of industries, including medical devices, industrial automation, supply chain automation, and home automation.

Here, we take pride in our ability to develop products that address modern needs and challenges. Through our smart home automation products, we specialize in creating connected home devices that simplify people's lives by facilitating communication between their homes and loved ones. To provide a seamless and intuitive user experience, all our products offer smartphone-enabled solutions and voice assistant control such as Alexa, Google, and Siri.

Our QMS is ISO 9001:2015 Certified!

Our medical devices use AI algorithms to identify pre-existing conditions or potential health problems. Our smart industry automation products use machine learning algorithms to optimize processes and enhance efficiency. Our supply chain automation solutions leverage deep learning techniques to provide real-time data insights and optimize inventory management.

We are committed to delivering innovative and reliable Smart IoT products that make your life easier and more connected. Whether you need smart water management products, smart city management products, smart lawns, smart parks, or smart building solutions, we have you covered.

Voni SmartIoT is passionate about using technology to create tangible objects that facilitate human interaction and improve people's lives. Our focus on innovation and excellence has earned us recognition, including the "Best Home Automation Product of the Year 2022" award. With our broad range of expertise, we are poised to lead the way in the exciting and ever-evolving field of IoT and artificial intelligence.
