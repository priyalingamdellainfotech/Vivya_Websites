Voyance ECG

Description

Voyance ECG is a portable, palm-sized ECG machine designed to effortlessly monitor your heart. Our innovative device is capable of generating ECG reports without internet connectivity through Voyance WiFi. Voyance ECG has an intuitive mobile application available for both Android and iOS platforms. Within just 15 seconds, it generates a comprehensive 12-lead simultaneous ECG report, quickly detecting abnormalities. Additionally, Voyance ECG features auto-detection of 11 arrhythmias, ensuring comprehensive heart health assessment. When further assessment of the ECG report is required, our support team facilitates sharing the report with Voyance's team of cardiologists. Within a remarkable 4-hour turnaround time, a validated report from our expert cardiologists is received. All ECG data is securely stored in the cloud, ensuring accessibility for future assessments anytime, anywhere. Utilizing IoT technology, Voyance seamlessly connects healthcare professionals worldwide, enabling live streaming of ECG data over extended periods to detect intermittent arrhythmias.
