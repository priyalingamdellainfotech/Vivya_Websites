The Ultimate Mobile Document Scanning Solution

Vivya Scans is an intuitive and sophisticated mobile document scanning application that simplifies document management. Our innovative solution makes the arduous process of scanning, editing, and sharing an effortless method without having to lug around a bulky traditional scanner.

With Vivya Scans, you can swiftly scan a range of documents, including ID cards, passports, and QR codes, with our high-quality OCR technology and save them in your preferred format in a fraction of a second, making the process seamless and efficient.

Added functionalities

You can also edit your scanned documents with a few taps, adding markups, signatures, and more. Need to compress a large file? No problem, Vivya Scans lets you quickly reduce document size. And if you are working with multiple files, you can easily merge them into one with our merging feature.

This product is ideal for those who need to stay organized and keep track of essential documents. It can be used for both personal and professional purposes, allowing users to quickly store, access, and share their documentation from anywhere.

How can it help businesses?

Vivya Scans helps businesses manage documents cost-effectively and efficiently. By eliminating costly equipment and physical storage space, businesses can save on expenses and increase productivity.

  • Enhanced document organization and improved accessibility
  • Reduce delays and streamline workflows
  • Reduce carbon footprint and promote sustainability
  • Save time, money, and resources

How can it help individuals?

Digitizing and storing important documents on mobile devices can be especially helpful for artists, writers, researchers, etc. By digitizing their original artwork, manuscripts, or research notes, they can easily store and access them from anywhere at any time, making it easier to share their work with others and collaborate with colleagues.

It helps to protect the original documents from damage or loss, as they can be stored securely in the cloud or on a backup drive.

  • Cost-effective solution
  • Reduce paper copies
  • Reduce clutter and save space in homes
  • Access from anywhere and at anytime
  • Readily scan and store important documents on their mobile devices
  • Save time by avoiding travel to make physical copies
