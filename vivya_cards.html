Vivya Cards, a business card scanner application for Android and iOS allows you to integrate any business card and deftly transfer the information to your phone contacts. Our app stands as a comprehensive solution for business networking where the user can save contacts with a single touch. The new-fangled AI and ML technology make the user communicate effortlessly with their collaborators and associates. The sophisticated OCR technology has been integrated into our application for better performance.

Vivya Cards for Android and iOS further allows the user to quickly scan and save information on Google or Apple cloud. It enables the user to translate the card data into 8 international languages without a hitch thus achieving exceptional accuracy. Being an expedient tool for entrepreneurs, retailers, stakeholders, and marketing executives, the app enhances the productive interaction in heterogenous coactions.
