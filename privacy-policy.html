Privacy Policy

Vezita Healthcare is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website vezita.com and use our services. By accessing or using our website and services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details when you voluntarily provide it to us through web forms, surveys, or other interactions.

Automatically Collected Data

Our website may collect information automatically, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage data when you interact with our website.

▸ Facilitating content posting in Facebook groups to improve relevance and quality.

▸ Analyzing how users interact with our app to improve its functionality.

▸ We will not disclose or share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

How We Use Your Information

▸ We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • ▸ Provide, operate, and maintain our website and services
  • ▸ Improve, personalize, and expand our website and services
  • ▸ Understand and analyze how you use our website and services
  • ▸ Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, including for customer service, updates, and marketing purposes
  • ▸ Process your transactions and manage your orders
  • ▸ Send you SMS messages with your consent, including marketing and promotional messages

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties without your consent, except as described below:

"No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties"

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

SMS Disclosures

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages from us. The messages may include marketing and promotional content. You can opt-out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Opt-In: You will receive SMS messages only if you have opted in through our web forms or other methods where we have clearly stated the terms and obtained your consent.

By providing a telephone number and submitting this form you are consenting to be contacted by SMS text message. Message & data rates may apply. You can reply STOP to opt-out of further messaging.

Opt-Out: To stop receiving SMS messages, you can reply STOP to any message or contact us at info@vezitahealth.com

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our website and hold certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our website.
